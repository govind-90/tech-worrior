COVID-19 & Its SYMPTOMS

Causes Of Covid-19

Corona Virus disease (Covid-19) is a respiratory disease caused by the infection of a new strain of corona virus SARS-CoV-2(Severe Acute respiratory Syndrome-Corona virus -2).

Symptoms Of Covid-19

Accccording To Word Health Organisation (WHO) The symptoms For COVID-19 Can be Categorised as Follow:

Common Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Fatigue

Symotoms That are seen less commonly but may be seen in some patiens:

  • loss of smell and taste
  • conjunctivitis (pink eyes)
  • Headache
  • Sore throat
  • Nausea
  • Diarrhea
  • Chill or dizziness
  • Muscle And joint Pain
  • Skin rashes

Severe Symptoms:

  • High temprature above 38 degree Celcius
  • Shortness Of Breath
  • Persistent pain in the Chest
  • Loss Of appetite
  • Confusion

Precautions To Prevent Covid-19

  • Wear Mask Always Covering The Nose And Mouth Double Mask if the situation requires.
  • Maintain a social distance of atleast 6 feet between each other
  • Sanitize with alcohal-rub/wash hand with soap regularly
  • Avoid Touching Eyes, nose, or mouth
  • Avoid large gathering and crowds
  • If havind fever, cough, or feeling unwell, consult a doctor.
  • Get vaccinated
